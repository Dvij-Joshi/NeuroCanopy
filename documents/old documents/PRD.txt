1. Executive Summary
NeuroCanopy is an AI-driven educational ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between academic potential and performance. Unlike traditional study aids that merely organize tasks, NeuroCanopy functions as a "Digital Guardian"—actively enforcing discipline through device-level control while optimizing learning retention through bio-mimetic visualization (The Knowledge Tree). The platform integrates a strict mobile companion app with an intelligent web dashboard to manage distractions, automate scheduling, and conduct rigorous AI-powered assessments.
2. Problem Statement
Current educational tools fail to address the three primary causes of academic underperformance in the digital age:
1.	Digital Fragmentation: Students lose an average of 3-4 hours daily to social media distractions and context switching.
2.	Static Planning: Traditional calendars do not adapt to real-time delays ("burnout") or sudden exam announcements, leading to "schedule collapse" and panic.
3.	Passive Learning: Students often read without retaining. There is a lack of active recall mechanisms (like oral vivas) available on demand to verify concept mastery.
3. Solution Overview
NeuroCanopy resolves these issues by deploying a Closed-Loop Productivity System:
•	Enforcement: It removes the option of distraction by locking the digital environment during study hours.
•	Adaptability: It utilizes AI to ingest schedules from informal channels (WhatsApp) and restructure study plans dynamically based on "Panic" or "Smooth" states.
•	Verification: It replaces passive reading with active "Voice Vivas," using AI to grade verbal proficiency and conceptual understanding.
________________________________________
4. Project Scope
4.1 In-Scope
•	Android Admin Application: A non-uninstallable companion app with "Draw Over" and "Usage Access" permissions for enforcement.
•	Web-Based Command Center: A React-based dashboard for analytics, the Knowledge Tree visualization, and the Redemption Store.
•	AI Backend: Integration of LLMs (Gemini/GPT) for Syllabus Parsing, Viva generation, and Speech Analysis.
•	Real-Time Database: Firebase implementation for instant sync between the Mobile blocker and Web dashboard.
4.2 Out-of-Scope (Phase 1)
•	iOS Application (Due to OS-level restriction limitations).
•	Physical Hardware Integration (Smart wearables).

5. Detailed Feature Specifications
I. The Mobile Companion (Android)
Designed for high-accountability enforcement and data ingestion.
•	Intelligent Schedule Parsing:
o	Utilizes notification access to analyze communication platforms (e.g., WhatsApp groups) for keywords related to exams, deadlines, or schedules.
o	Action: Automatically prompts the user to add detected dates to the central database or upload relevant schedule documents immediately.
•	Distraction Management Protocol:
o	Enforces a strict "Focus Mode" during scheduled study sessions or if daily targets are missed.
o	Blocking Logic: Temporarily restricts access to social media and entertainment applications. Includes a "buff" allowance (5-10 minute emergency windows) to ensure safety without compromising focus.
•	Persistent Accountability Alerts:
o	Utilizes "Draw Over Other Apps" permissions to display intrusive reminders if the user neglects critical tasks (e.g., failing to upload a syllabus).
o	Escalation: Alerts become persistent and cannot be dismissed if the user repeatedly ignores tasks.
•	Device Administration Security:
o	Operates as a Device Admin app to prevent unauthorized uninstallation during active academic terms, ensuring consistent adherence to the study regimen.
________________________________________
II. The Intelligent Web Platform (AI Dashboard)
The central command center for analytics, visualization, and learning management.
1. Dynamic Pacing Engine
•	Real-Time Status Indicators: Categorizes user preparation into three states:
o	Smooth: User is on track with the syllabus.
o	Alert: Mild backlog detected; schedule compresses slightly.
o	Panic Mode: Critical time deficit detected. The AI automatically ignores low-weightage topics and restructures the schedule to focus exclusively on high-yield topics and past paper trends for the nearest exam.
2. Comprehensive Analytics Suite
•	Concept Mastery Index: Tracks the percentage of concepts mastered versus the total syllabus.
•	Effort Estimation: meaningful study hours derived from "Focus Mode" duration and completion of test topics.
•	Viva Performance Metrics: Displays average viva scores and session frequency.
•	Review Radar: Identifies "At-Risk" topics that require immediate review based on forgetting curves.
•	Subject Proficiency Graph: A radar chart visualizing strengths and weaknesses across all subjects.
3. Visualized Knowledge Graph (The Tree)
•	Organic Progress Visualization: Represents the syllabus as a living tree.
o	Growth: New topics appear as buds/small leaves.
o	Mastery: Completed topics turn into vibrant green leaves.
o	Decay Mechanics (Spaced Repetition): If a topic is not revised, its leaf turns brown and eventually falls, visually signaling memory decay.
•	Scale: The complexity and size of the tree dynamically adjust to match the total number of topics in the user's curriculum.
4. Adaptive Scheduler
•	Quantum Calendar: A dynamic calendar that updates in real-time. If a study block is missed or a new exam is announced, the AI automatically reshuffles the remaining tasks to ensure coverage without manual replanning.
5. Syllabus Commander & Resource Vault
•	Curriculum Tracking: Displays total courses, progress grades, and topic-level breakdown.
•	Resource Library (New): Allows users to upload learning materials (PDFs, PPTs, handwritten notes). The AI parses these files to populate the "Topics" list automatically.
________________________________________
III. AI Assessment & Tutor Engine
The interactive layer for testing and concept reinforcement.
1. Secure Assessment Environment (Voice Viva & Quiz)
•	Anti-Cheat Integrity: Enforces a locked browser/window state during assessments.
o	Violation Logic: Copy-pasting, tab switching, or window minimizing results in immediate session cancellation.
o	Punitive Measures: Repeated violations trigger a system-wide lock on leisure apps/websites via the mobile companion.
•	AI Viva Examiner: Conducts oral examinations based on the schedule.
o	Speech Analysis: Evaluates not just the answer accuracy, but also speech clarity, confidence levels, and pronunciation.
2. Interactive Learning Assistant (New)
•	Doubt Resolution: Provides an interface for users to ask clarification questions on specific topics before or after a viva session.
•	Summary Generation: Generates concise summaries of uploaded PDFs to aid in quick revision before "Panic Mode" sets in.
3. Gamification & Redemption (New)
•	Streak System: Tracks consecutive days of meeting study goals.
•	Redemption Store: Allows users to "buy back" lost privileges (like social media time) or restore "fallen leaves" by performing extra credit quizzes, balancing the strict punishment system with positive reinforcement.

________________________________________
6. Technical Architecture & Constraints
•	Frontend: React.js (Web), React Native/Kotlin (Android).
•	Backend: Firebase (Firestore, Cloud Functions, Authentication).
•	AI Integration: Google Gemini API (via MCP or Cloud Functions) for text parsing and speech-to-text analysis.
•	Security: strict Row-Level Security (RLS) on Firestore to ensure data privacy. API Keys managed via environment variables.
________________________________________
7. Success Metrics (KPIs)
The success of NeuroCanopy will be measured by:
1.	Retention Rate: Percentage of "Green Leaves" maintained on the Knowledge Tree week-over-week.
2.	Distraction Reduction: Decrease in "Social Media Usage" stats during active Focus Mode blocks.
3.	Viva Proficiency: Improvement in average Viva scores over a 30-day period.
